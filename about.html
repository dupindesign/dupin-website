    My name is Eduardo Motta and I am a seasoned graphic designer and video editor with international experience. I have crafted compelling visual designes that tell stories in Brazil, the United Arab Emirates, Singapore, Portugal and the United States of America. My global journey has not only shaped my creative approach, but also instilled in me a deep appreciation for a good narrative and cultural nuance in my work. My passion for story telling is matched by my attention to detail as I ensure that the projects I undertake are both visually unique and narratively engaging.
